I am a PhD candidate at the Department of CSE, IIT Bombay, since Autumn 2014. I am working under the guidance of Prof Uday Khedkar.

Research Interests

I am pursuing research in the area of compilers where I am applying Data Flow Techniques to extract important data flow information to smartly optimize a given C code, in ways which would otherwise not be possible. Please find more information here.

Research

My primary objective is to build suitable abstractions for data flow analysis and construct a new language around it, such that the data flow programs are much easier to express than using the contemporary methods.

My other interests include Congnitive Science and Philosophy, which keeps me buzy in my spare time.
